ABC Kiddy Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 10, 2025

Our Commitment to Privacy

ABC Kiddy is committed to providing a safe, educational experience for children. This privacy policy explains our practices regarding the collection and use of information within our app.

Information We Collect

ABC Kiddy is designed to be privacy-friendly. We do not collect any personal information from children using our app. The app does not:

  • Require account creation
  • Collect names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Track location data
  • Contain third-party advertising
  • Include in-app purchases

Usage Data

We collect anonymous usage data to help us improve the app, including:

  • Which features are used most frequently
  • How long users typically engage with the app
  • Any technical errors that occur

This data cannot be linked to individual users and is used solely to enhance the app experience.

Information Storage

Progress in games and learning activities is stored locally on your device. This data is not transmitted to our servers.

Parental Rights

As we do not collect personal information from children, there is no data to access, correct, or delete. However, parents with questions or concerns about our privacy practices can contact us at the email address below.
